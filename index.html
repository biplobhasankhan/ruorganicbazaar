Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, click the "Buy Now" button on any item you'd like to purchase, and you'll be redirected to our Facebook page where you can complete your order via message.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all areas within Rajshahi city. For locations outside Rajshahi, please contact us directly to check availability.

How do I know your fruits are truly organic?

All our fruits are grown using organic farming practices without chemical pesticides or fertilizers. We work directly with certified organic farmers and regularly test our products to ensure they meet organic standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bKash, Nagad, and other mobile banking services. Payment details will be provided when you place your order.

How fresh are your fruits?

Our fruits are harvested on the same day or a day before delivery to ensure maximum freshness. We never store fruits for long periods, guaranteeing you receive the freshest produce possible.
